# REVORY hybrid — known limitations

Status: active product-truth guardrail, updated 2026-07-13.

REVORY is self-service Revenue Leak Intelligence for high-ticket service businesses. It is not a CRM, inbox, autonomous follow-up agent, field-service system, accounting suite, job-costing system, project-management suite, generic BI product or consulting service.

## Launch boundaries

- Input is CSV/XLSX-first. Native connectors are not a launch claim.
- Quote Recovery financial values are estimated opportunity, never confirmed accounting loss or guaranteed recovery.
- Revenue Realization requires explicit job-level export-completeness declarations, unambiguous IDs, compatible currency and valid dates. Unsupported jobs are suppressed.
- Approved-change, underbilling and margin findings remain commercially gated until the corrected Sprint 9 corpus, independent review, customer validation and Pro security gate pass.
- Text can create a review candidate only. It cannot prove approval, performed scope or a financial gap.
- Growth segmentation requires minimum comparable samples and never becomes a rep/source performance ranking.
- The weekly decision is a deterministic review priority. Customer feedback is required to prove that it is useful.
- AI is optional and bounded. It may assist mapping/explanation; it does not calculate financial truth or authorize action.
- One-time Audit access expires and is limited to its contracted run count. Subscription capability follows explicit entitlements.

## External launch evidence still required

- final legal entity/CNPJ, privacy/security/support contacts and qualified review of Terms, Privacy, DPA, subprocessors, refund/cancellation, tax and jurisdiction;
- Stripe test-mode checkout, event replay/reordering, subscription revocation and portal E2E;
- Google OAuth, Resend delivery, final domain and managed-secret parity;
- production database migration, cron, monitoring/alerts, restore drill with RPO/RTO and independent DAST/pentest;
- customer-shaped data and paid/usefulness evidence.

Until those gates pass, technical previews and target prices are not evidence that Growth or Pro is sellable.

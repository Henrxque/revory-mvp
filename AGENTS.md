## Product Guardrails

- Premium
- Self-service
- MedSpa-first
- Revenue leak-first
- Evidence-first
- Estimated revenue at risk
- Data quality before claims
- One main offer per client at the start
- Short guided flow
- Short triage
- Minimal low-cost AI
- Revenue-risk dashboard

Avoid turning the product into:

- a universal chatbot
- a CRM
- a full inbox
- a BI suite
- a scheduling system
- a revenue cycle management system
- an enterprise control center
- a manual service disguised as software
- an AI sales agent
- a clinical or diagnostic product

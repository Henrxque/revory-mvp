## Product Guardrails

- Premium
- Self-service
- MedSpa-first
- Booking-first
- One main offer per client at the start
- Short guided flow
- Short triage
- Minimal low-cost AI
- Revenue-first dashboard

Avoid turning the product into:

- a universal chatbot
- a CRM
- a full inbox
- an enterprise control center
- a manual service disguised as software

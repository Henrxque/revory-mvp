The Problem

Your paid lead flow is leaking revenue before it becomes booked appointments.

Most MedSpas do not lose first on lead volume. They lose after the lead comes in. The money is already being spent. The loss happens when paid demand cools off, follow-up gets inconsistent, or too much friction sits between interest and the appointment booked.

Slow response kills intent

When a paid lead waits too long, booking intent drops fast. Premium demand does not stay warm for your team.

Lead handling gets inconsistent

Different reps respond differently, qualify differently, and follow up with different urgency. That inconsistency costs real appointments.

Too much friction sits between lead and appointment

Too many steps, too much back-and-forth, and too little structure turn paid demand into lost momentum.

Paid demand gets wasted

If you are already paying to create demand, losing those leads before they book is one of the fastest ways to burn budget without seeing revenue back.

Revenue visibility stays blurry

Without clear booked proof, it is harder to see what paid demand is producing, what is getting stuck, and where revenue is leaking.

Yes. You sign up, add the booking data you need, confirm your main offer, and activate through a short structured setup. No implementation project required.

+
You can start with the booking data needed to make booked proof and revenue visible. REVORY Seller is designed to stay simple to configure without a heavy technical rollout.

Absolutely. REVORY Seller works with email alone. SMS is optional, but the core booking path does not depend on it.

No. REVORY Seller is not a CRM, not a universal inbox, and not an open chatbot. It is a narrow booking acceleration system built to help MedSpas convert paid leads with more speed and better booking execution.
